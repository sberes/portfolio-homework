ABOUT ME

I manage my youngest daughter’s career, I am a social media manager, I maintain three websites (*more on that in a bit), I run a non-profit, Socks for Souls, Inc., I assist with taxes and business issues as a virtual assistant, work at the Palos Verdes Unified School District, and I design and sell mermaid tails.


I am new to front-end web development. In the past I have used templates to design and maintain websites. Since the ’90’s when HTML was fairly new, I always wanted to learn how to create an on-line presence from scratch. Military life and being a full-time parent, kept me from my curiosities until recently when I decided to join General Assembly and learn Front-end Development.


In my spare time, I enjoy walking my dogs, photography, swimming, hiking, and creating. I am a cancer survivor, I have survived two heart surgeries and I am a empty-nester. Biggest accomplishment 2016 swimming pier-to-pier!
